Understanding the Watch Mechanism

A Perpetual Quest

At the Heart of Our Philosophy

At Chronos Elegance, we believe that a watch is more than a timekeeping device; it is a symbol of tradition, a testament to craftsmanship, and a personal statement of style. Our philosophy is rooted in the appreciation of timeless elegance and enduring craftsmanship. Each timepiece in our collection is carefully selected to represent the pinnacle of design and functionality.

At the core of excellence, emblematic words, integrated know-how, and a singular vision of watchmaking.

Watches are fascinating devices that combine art, science, and engineering. In this guide, we'll explore the intricate world of watch mechanisms.

Gear Train

The gear train transmits the stored energy from the mainspring to the balance wheel, controlling the release of energy.

Balance Wheel

The balance wheel, in combination with the hairspring, oscillates back and forth at a constant rate, dividing time into equal segments.
