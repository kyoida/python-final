The Story of Savoir Timepieces

Nestled in the Swiss Alps, Savoir was born from a tradition of precision and artistry. Its name reflects a belief that a watch captures time's essence. Founded in 1923 by Jean-Luc Girardin, Savoir watches became symbols of adventure, elegance, and enduring style.
